Gingerbread Babycakes
Yields 18 cakes
Bake 30 minutes at 360F

Dry Ingredients mix 2 cups flour with
Instant Coffee		2 tbsp
Dry cocoa		1/4 cup
Ground Ginger		5 tsp
Baking Powder		1/2 tsp
Salt			1 tsp
Freshly ground pepper	1 tsp (optional)

Beat 1/2 lb butter (2 sticks) & 1 cup Brown Sugar until smooth
Add 4 eggs & beat until thoroughly mised
Beat in 1/4 cup fresh ginger & 2 cups (620 g) molasses on medium speed
Incorporate dry ingredients
Pour mixture into greased & floured cake pans
