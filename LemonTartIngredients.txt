Pastry Dough:

90 g (3 ounces) unsalted butter, cut into pieces
15 g (1 tablespoon) vegetable oil (I used canola)
60 g (3 tablespoons) water
15 g (1 tablespoon) sugar
1/8 teaspoon salt
150 g (5oz, or 1 slightly-rounded cup) flour
Preheat the oven to 410º F (210º C).


Lemon Curd:

1/2 cup (125 ml) freshly-squeezed lemon juice
grated zest of one lemon, preferably unsprayed
1/2 cup (100 g) sugar
6 tablespoons (85 g) butter, salted or unsalted, cut into bits
2 large eggs
2 large egg yolks
